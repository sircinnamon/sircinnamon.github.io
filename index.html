Spell Templates

A tool for calculating spell attack hitboxes in D&D 5e and similar tabletop RPGs. Built from scratch on pure javascript, utilizing HTML5 Canvases and a whole lot of math.

Spell templates operates fully client side and so can be hosted on GutHub pages as fully static content. It's also a fully compliant Progressive Web App and can be installed to most phones and used fully offline.
